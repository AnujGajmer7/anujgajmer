About Laxmi God!

She is the goddess of wealth, fortune, power, beauty, fertility
and prosperity, and associated with Maya ("Illusion"). Along with
Parvati and Saraswati, she forms the Tridevi of Hindu goddesses. Padma (Lotus),
Shankha (conch), Discus, Gada (mace), Jnana Mudra, Abhaya Mudra, Varadamudra, Gold.
